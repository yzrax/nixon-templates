Chapter I

The first drop

It begins with a single bean, carefully selected from the highlands of Ethiopia. Every pour tells a story of origin, patience, and craft.

Chapter II

The extraction

Water meets grounds at precisely 93°C. The pressure builds. Rich crema forms as the first dark rivulets emerge — liquid gold in motion.

Chapter III

The bloom

Steam rises as the espresso fills the cup. Aroma compounds unfold — chocolate, stone fruit, a whisper of jasmine. This is the scent of morning.

Chapter IV

The milk & canvas

Velvet-smooth microfoam cascades into the brew. The barista's hand guides the pour — steady, intentional, practiced over thousands of cups.

Chapter V

The art revealed

A rosetta forms on the surface. No two patterns are exactly alike — each cup a unique collaboration between the barista, the bean, and the moment.

The finished cup

Made for you

From the first sip to the last, this is what we do. Welcome to Aura & Bean. Your table is ready.
